# Legal Documents for RAW Gallery

## Required Documents Created

### 1. End User License Agreement (EULA.md)
**Purpose**: Defines terms and conditions for using the app
**Key Points**:
- License grant and restrictions
- Intellectual property rights
- Photo library access terms
- Apple App Store compliance
- Limitation of liability
- Termination conditions

### 2. Privacy Policy (PRIVACY_POLICY.md) 
**Purpose**: Explains data collection and privacy practices
**Key Points**:
- **No data collection statement**
- Photo library access explanation
- Local processing only
- No third-party services
- GDPR and CCPA compliance
- Children's privacy protection

## Implementation Requirements

### App Store Connect Setup
1. **Host both documents** on a publicly accessible website
2. **Add URLs** to App Store Connect:
   - Privacy Policy URL: `https://yourwebsite.com/privacy-policy`
   - Terms of Use URL: `https://yourwebsite.com/eula`
   - Support URL: `https://yourwebsite.com/support`

## Important Notes

### Privacy-First Design
Both documents emphasize that RAW Gallery:
- Collects **zero personal data**
- Processes everything **locally on device**
- Uses **no third-party services**
- Requires **only photo library access**

### Apple Requirements
- Privacy Policy **required** for App Store submission
- EULA **recommended** for legal protection
- Both must be **publicly accessible** via web links
- Must **accurately reflect** app behavior

### Legal Protection
These documents provide protection by:
- Limiting developer liability
- Defining user responsibilities
- Clarifying data handling practices
- Establishing terms of service
