‚ÑπÔ∏è App Overview

Anytime Recall is a comprehensive barcode scanning app that's designed to make it easy to stay informed on product safety. Scan or enter any barcode to begin checking for recalls across multiple US government databases with ease.

  • Add barcodes by camera, photo, or typing the UPC, VIN, etc.
  • Recall searches performed with key databases: FDA (food, medicine, and devices) and NHTSA (vehicles)
  • View and share detailed recall information
  • Easily back up locally or with iCloud

üì∑ Scanning Methods

Add one or more barcodes at a time from a single photo or camera scan. Select the barcodes you want to add from the list and repeat as needed.

  • Manual entry supported (choose QR or Code 128 for alphanumeric)
  • Real-time validation to reduce typos
